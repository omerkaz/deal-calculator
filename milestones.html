Milestone 001

The Foundation — Core CRM

Everything Hüseyin needs to stop managing patients from memory. A structured database, a visual pipeline, and the integrations that feed it — built from the ground up.

Slice 01 — Scaffolding

Project Bootstrap & Supabase Schema

Stand up the React + TypeScript application with Supabase. Design the core database schema: patients, statuses, packages, payments, notes, and documents. Configure Row Level Security policies and authentication. This is the skeleton everything else hangs on.

Postgres Schema RLS Policies Auth Setup React Scaffold
Slice 02 — Patient Records

Patient Profiles & Lifecycle States

The heart of the CRM. Each patient gets a rich profile: personal details, package type, treatment timeline, blood test documents, protocol PDFs, and notes. The status engine tracks 10+ states — from Lead through Active Treatment to Completed or Cold — with timestamps and transition history.

Patient CRUD 10+ States Document Upload Transition History
Slice 03 — Pipeline View

Visual Pipeline & Lead Tracking

A Kanban-style board where every patient is a card moving through stages. Drag to reclassify. Filter by package type, date range, or assigned status. At a glance, Hüseyin sees exactly where every patient stands — no scrolling through chat histories.

Kanban Board Smart Filters Drag & Drop
Slice 04 — Payments

Payment Tracking & Status

Track every payment against every patient: PayPal transactions, international bank transfers, partial payments, and refunds. Package selection auto-populates expected amounts. Payment status updates sync to the patient's lifecycle — paid means active, overdue triggers a flag.

PayPal Bank Transfer Overdue Alerts Payment History
Slice 05 — Lead Capture

ManyChat Webhook Integration

When someone comments a keyword on an Instagram reel, ManyChat captures the lead. A webhook pushes that data straight into the CRM — name, Instagram handle, email, phone (with country code validation). No manual entry. No leads lost in DMs.

Webhook Endpoint Instagram Data Phone Validation Auto-Create Lead
Slice 06 — Dashboard

Analytics Dashboard

The command center. Active client count, pipeline breakdown by stage, monthly revenue, conversion rate (lead → patient), and package distribution. Clean cards with the warm design system. Hüseyin opens this once a day and knows exactly where his business stands.

Revenue Charts Pipeline Breakdown Conversion Rate Live Metrics
Foundation Complete — Automation Begins
Milestone 002

The Intelligence — Automation & Polish

The CRM tracks. This milestone makes it act. Automated follow-ups, smart reminders at treatment milestones, scheduling that books itself, and the polish that turns a tool into a trusted system.

Slice 01 — Email Engine

Automated Email Sequences

When a lead enters the system, a welcome email fires immediately. Day 3: a follow-up. Day 7: another nudge. Day 12: if no response, mark cold. No manual tracking — the system moves leads forward or lets them go. SPF/DKIM configuration ensures nothing lands in spam.

Welcome Email Drip Sequence SPF/DKIM Auto-Cold Mark
Slice 02 — Smart Reminders

Treatment Milestone Reminders

The system knows the treatment timeline. Blood test not uploaded after 5 days? Reminder. Week 6 check-in approaching? Hüseyin gets a notification and the patient gets a message. End-of-programme review due? Scheduled automatically. VIP clients get monthly check-ins. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Blood Test Pending Week 6 Check-in End Review VIP Monthly
Slice 03 — Scheduling

Consultation Calendar Integration

Patients book consultations through available time slots synced with Hüseyin's calendar. Timezone-aware — critical for an international practice spanning Turkey, Thailand, and beyond. Confirmation emails, rescheduling, and no-show tracking built in.

Calendar Sync Timezone Aware Confirmations No-Show Tracking
Slice 04 — Protocols

PDF Protocol Delivery

Treatment protocols — supplement plans, 90-day guides, blood test interpretations — stored in the system and delivered directly from the patient record. No more digging through Google Drive folders. One click: generate, attach, send.

PDF Generation Direct Delivery Document Vault
Slice 05 — Landing Page

Landing Page Optimization

The existing huseyinajuz.com on Netlify gets sharpened. Booking form improvements, phone country code validation (a recurring pain point), multi-language flag support for international clients, and tighter conversion tracking from landing page → lead in CRM.

Multi-Language Country Codes Conversion Tracking Mobile Optimized
Slice 06 — Deliverability

Email Deliverability & Trust

Emails going to spam kills the entire automation engine. This slice configures SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records for mrhus@huseyinacuz.com, sets up proper sending infrastructure, and verifies inbox placement. The foundation the email sequences depend on.

SPF Records DKIM Signing DMARC Policy Inbox Testing
Slice 07 — Multi-Language

International Client Support

Hüseyin's clients span continents. Language flags on patient records, locale-aware date and currency formatting, and template support for communications in multiple languages. The system respects that not every patient reads English.

Language Flags Locale Formatting Template i18n
